~What is Ashen Waters?

Ashen Waters is an up-and-coming adventure game created in RPG Maker MZ. It stars actor Mira, as she travels the world discovering the secrets of the waters she thought were hers to trust. When the ashes fall, calamity awaits those in the waters, and the desctruction of cities nearby. Will you figure out where the ashes come from, and what to make of them?

~Is the game available now?

Yes, the game can be played right now! You can access it using this link or at the Game tab at the top of the website. However, the game is not fully completed yet, and there will be bugs or things that are not yet implemented into the game yet. More updates of content will gradually roll out.
