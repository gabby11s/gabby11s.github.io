What will you do in freshman year?

You will be put into a rotation system, rotating between 4 IT classes every quarter of the year. The classes you will take freshman year revolve around software, hardware, networking, cybersecurity, and computer programming to get a well-rounded foundation of IT.

For most of freshman year, you will prepare to take the CompTIA IT Fundamentals (ITF+) certification. York Tech offers lots of certifications for students, which help you stand out in the workforce since you can be certified and experienced earlier than other candidates!

Some After-School Clubs & Activities for IT students

  • CyberPatriot - National Air & Space Association Cyber Education Program (Cybersecurity). Student-led club that teaches cybersecurity and network security
  • Esports - Members play multiplayer video games against other high school teams. Games included are Overwatch, League of Legends, Super Smash Brothers, Splatoon 3. Mario Kart 8, and Rocket League
  • Game Development Club - Members design and test games of all types, but mostly video games
  • SkillsUSA - National organization that develops leadership and occupational skills. Can participate in IT releated competitions
  • TSA - The Technology Student Association (TSA) is national organization for students interested in competing in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) competitions
